Meath County Council Planning Department
 


This record includes details of plans, elevations and mapping submitted with Planning applications received from 1st October 2012 to date. Supporting documentation lodged with the Planning application is only available for inspection at the offices of the Planning Authority. There may be a delay between the lodgement date of the application and the availability of the scanned document with respect to current planning applications. The Planning register held by Meath County Council consists of electronic and hard copy records held in the office of the Planning Authority. Meath County Council staff are available to assist any person seeking to conduct a planning search at its offices at Buvinda House, Dublin Road, Navan, Co.
